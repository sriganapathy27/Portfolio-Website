ZEN HEAVEN

  • The Zen Heaven House embodies tranquility and modern sophistication, seamlessly blending contemporary architecture with principles of harmony and balance.
  • Its design emphasizes simplicity and natural integration, featuring clean lines, open spaces, and an abundance of natural light.
  • Wooden accents, stone textures, and lush greenery create a serene and earthy atmosphere, while water elements like reflective pools or fountains enhance the sense of calm.
  • The interior spaces are meticulously organized to promote mindfulness, with minimalistic furnishings and soft, neutral tones fostering relaxation.
  • The Zen Heaven House is more than a residence; it is a sanctuary that inspires a peaceful lifestyle, merging modern luxury with the timeless wisdom of Zen philosophy.

GOLDEN VEIL RESIDENCE

  • This building showcases a modern architectural design with a blend of elegance and functionality.
  • The facade is framed by a striking golden-accented curve that softens the structure's edges and adds a sense of fluidity to the overall form.
  • Vertical wooden slats dominate the design, providing privacy while allowing natural light and ventilation to permeate the interior spaces, creating a warm, textured appearance.
  • Strategically placed rectangular windows break the rhythm of the slats, reinforcing the contemporary aesthetic.
  • The integration of greenery through planters at the ground level enhances the building's connection to nature, softening its urban presence.
  • A thoughtfully chosen material palette of concrete, wood, and metal, highlighted with golden accents, reflects durability, luxury, and sophistication. The semi-open ground floor creates an inviting entrance, blending seamlessly with the minimalist design.
